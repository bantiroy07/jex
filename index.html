Our Story

The Waves Restaurant is a family-friendly restaurant in Ramgarh Cantonment, known for its high-quality North Indian, Chinese, and Mughlai dishes. Perfect for parties, gatherings, and casual dining, we strive to provide an unforgettable culinary experience.

Established with a passion for flavor and community, every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and authentic spices.
